Jambalaya

All Casserole Recipes

Ingredients

Switch to Steps
  • 1/2 lb Smoked Sausage
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper
  • 1 Celery
  • 1 28 oz can Diced Tomatoes
  • 10 1/2 oz Chicken Broth
  • 1 cup White Rice, uncooked
  • 1 Tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Dried Thyme Leaves
  • 1/4 tsp Ground Red Pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 12 oz Frozen Shrimp
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 3/4 lb Smoked Ham

Steps

Switch to Ingredients
  1. Preheat oven to 350F.

  2. Spray 13"x9" baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.

  3. Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat until hot.

  4. Add ham and sausage.

  5. Cook and stir 5 minutes or until sausage is lightly browned on both sides.

  6. Remove from skillet and place in prepared dish.

  7. Place onion, bell pepper, celery, and garlic in same skillet.

  8. Cook and stir 3 minutes.

  9. Add to ham & sausage mixture.

  10. Combine tomatoes with juice, broth, rice, Worcestershire sauce, salt,
    thyme and black and red peppers in same skillet.

  11. Bring to boil over high heat.

  12. Reduce heat to low and simmer 3 minutes.

  13. Pour over ham & sausage mixture and stir until combined.

  14. Cover lightly with foil and bake 45 minutes or until rice is almost tender.

  15. Remove from oven and place shrimp on top of the rice mixture.

  16. Bake, uncovered for 10 minutes or until shrimp are pink and opaque.
