Deep-Pan Vegetable Pizza

All Pizza Recipes

Ingredients

Switch to Steps

Base

  • 1 tsp Dry Yeast
  • 12 oz Plain (All Purpose) Flour
  • Large pinch of Salt
  • 1/2 cup Olive Oil

Topping

  • 8 oz can of Chopped Tomatoes, drained
  • 2 Tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 2 cloves Garlic, crushed
  • 20 leaves Fresh Spinach
  • 4 oz White Mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • 1 Red Pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1/2 Red Onion, chopped
  • 4 oz Zucchini, slized
  • 1/2 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1/2 tsp Dried Thyme
  • 4 oz Mozarella, grated

Steps

Switch to Ingredients
  1. Put the yeast and 2/3 cup of lukewarm water into a bowl and stir.

  2. Leave for 5-10 minutes, until frothy.

  3. Sift the flour and salt into another bowl and make a well in the center.

  4. Mix in the yeast liquid and oil to form a dough.

  5. Knead the dough for 5 minutes or until the dough is smooth and elastic.

  6. Lightly brush a bowl with oil.

  7. Put the dough in the bowl, cover with oiled cling wrap,
    and leave in a warm place for 1 hour or until it has doubled in size.

  8. Preheat oven to 400F.

  9. Knead the dough briefly, then roll out or press to fit a 12" loose-bottomed tin.

  10. Mix together the tomatoes nd tomato paste, then spread them over the base.

  11. Heat half the oil in a saucepan, add the garlic and spinach
    and cook, stirring, for 3 minutes or until the spinach wilts.

  12. Drain and arrange over the tomatoes.

  13. Top the pizza with the remaing vegetables, herbs and mozarella cheese.

  14. Drizzle over the rest of the oil.

  15. Bake for 30 minutes, until golden.
