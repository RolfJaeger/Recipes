Mushroom Pizza

All Pizza Recipes

Ingredients

Switch to Steps

Base

  • 1 1/2 tsp Dry Yeast
  • 1 cup Warm Water
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 3 cups Plain (All Purpose) Flour
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 3 Tbsp Olive Oil

Topping

  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 10 1/2 oz Jar of Salsa Dip of Choice
  • 1 Onion, sliced
  • Mushrooms, sliced
  • Tasty or Cheddar Cheese, grated
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Sprinkle of Oregano Leaves

Steps

Switch to Ingredients
  1. Dissolve the yeast in the warm water with the sugar.

  2. Let stand until it foams .

  3. Sift the flour into a bowl and add the yeast mixture, olive oil and salt.

  4. Mix to combine and set aside until doubled in size.

  5. Preheat oven to 430F.

  6. Turn out onto a floured board and knead to form a soft dough.

  7. Press a piece of the dough, about the size of a tennis ball, onto a pizza tray
    (or a large baking dish).

  8. Brush the pizza base with the olive oil, spread liberally with salsa,
    , then add the onion and mushrooms.

  9. Drizzle with more olive oil annd cover liberally with the cheese, salt and pepper.

  10. Sprinkle with the oregano leaves.

  11. Place in oven for 20 - 25 minutes.
