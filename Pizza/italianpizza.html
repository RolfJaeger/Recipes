Ingredients

Switch to Steps

Base

  • 1 lb Plain (All Purpose) Flour
  • 1 cup Warm Water
  • 1 Tbsp Dry Yeast
  • 2 oz Carrot Juice
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

Tomato Sauce

  • 3 Tomatoes, sliced
  • Juice of 2 Tomatoes
  • 2 Basil Leaves, chopped
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • Pinch of Oregano

Toppings of Choice, e.g.

  • Mushrooms
  • Salami or Pepperoni
  • Olives
  • Mozarella

Steps

Switch to Ingredients
  1. Make a well in the flour.

  2. Mix 2 oz of warm water with the yeast and pour into the well in the flour
    along with the carrot juice, remaining water and salt.

  3. Preheat oven to 430F.

  4. Mix together to form a dough and place in warm area to rest and rise.

  5. For the sauce, cook the tomatoes, tomato juice, basil, garlic and oregano,
    simmering until the sauce has reduced.

  6. Press a piece of the dough, about the size of a tennis ball, onto a pizza tray
    (or a large baking dish).

  7. Spread the sauce onto the pizza dough.

  8. Add your toppings.

  9. Place in oven for 20 - 25 minutes.
