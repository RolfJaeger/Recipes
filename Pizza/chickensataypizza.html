Chicken Satay Pizza

All Pizza Recipes

Ingredients

Switch to Steps

Marinade

  • 1/2 cup Peanut Butter
  • 1/2 tsp Chili Powder
  • 2 Tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 Tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Ginger
  • 1/2 cup Coconut Milk

Pizza

  • 1 Ready-Made Pizza Base ((separate recipe))
  • 14 oz Chicken Thigh Filets, diced
  • 3/4 cup Satay Marinade
  • 1 Tbsp Peanut Oil
  • 4 Green Onions, sliced
  • 1 Carrot, grated
  • 3 oz Snow Peas, trimmed and sliced
  • 2 tsp Sesame Seeds
  • 1/4 cup Cilantro, chopped

Steps

Switch to Ingredients

Marinade

  1. Heat all ingredients in a pan, stir to combine, then allow to cool.

Pizza

  1. Preheat oven to 430F.

  2. Combine the chicken with 1/2 cup marinade in a bowl.

  3. Heat the oil in a large frying pan over medium to high heat.

  4. Cook the chicken for 5 minutes.

  5. Stir in the green onions, carrot and snow peas and cook for 1-2 minutes
    or until the snow peas turn bright green.

  6. Spread the remaining marinade over the pizza base and spoon the chicken over.

  7. Sprinkle with sesame seeds.

  8. Place on a baking tray and bake for 12-15 minutes.

  9. Top with the cilantro and cut into wedges.
