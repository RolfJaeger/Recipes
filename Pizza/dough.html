Basic Pizza Dough

All Pizza Recipes

Ingredients

Switch to Steps
  • 1 tsp Active Dry Yeast
  • pinch of Sugar
  • 1/4 warm water
  • 2 cups Plain (All Purpose) Flour
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 cup Olive Oil

Steps

Switch to Ingredients
  1. Place the yeast, sugar and water in a large bowl and mix to dissolve.

  2. Set aside in a warm, draught-free place for 5 minutes or until foamy.

  3. Place the flour and salt in another bowl and sift.

  4. Slowly pour in the oil and yeast mixture and form a rough dough.

  5. Turn the dough onto a lightly floured surfae and knead for 5 minutes
    or until soft and shiny. Add more flour if neccessary.

  6. Lightly oil a large bowl, then roll the dough around in the bowl to cover the surface with oil.

  7. Cover the bowl tighyly with plastic food wrap and place in a warm,
    draught-free place for 1 1/2 - 2 hours or until the dough has doubled in volume.

  8. Knock down and remove from the bowl.

  9. Knead briefly before using as desired.
