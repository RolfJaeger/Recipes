Red's Favorite Schnitzel

All Pork Recipes

Ingredients

  • 4 Boneless Pork Chops
  • 2 1/2 cups Bread Crumbs
  • 1 Broccoli Head
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 4 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 3 Tbsp Salted Butter
  • 1 Lemon, sliced

Steps

  1. Season the pork chops with salt and pepper.

  2. In a shallow medium bowl, combine the bread crumbs, garlic powder,
    paprika, and a pinch each of salt and pepper.

  3. Working with one chop at a time, press the pork into the bread crumbs,
    using your fist .

  4. Meanwile, heat the canola oil in a large, deep pot over medium heat.
    using your fist to pound the crumbs into the pork.

  5. Repeat with the remaining chops.

  6. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

  7. When the oil simmers, add 2 of the chops and cook until
    deep golden brown on both sides, 3 to 4 minutes per side.

  8. Transfer the schnitzels to a paper-,-lines plate to drain,
    and repeat for the remaining chops.

  9. Wipe the skilet clean and add the butter and lemon slices.

  10. Sear the lemon until golden on each side, about 1 minute per side.

  11. Remove the lemon from the skillet and add to the plate with the schnitzel.

  12. Place the broccoli in the same skillet and season with salt and pepper.

  13. Cook until charred on the edges and tender, about 5 minutes.
