Ingredients

Switch to Steps
  • 3 cups Flour
  • 1 cup Unsalted Butter
  • 2/3 cup Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 6 Egg Yolks
  • Cinemon/sugar mixture

Steps

Switch to Ingredients
  1. Combine all ingredients to a smooth dough.

  2. Cool for 1 hour.

  3. Preheat the oven to 350F
  4. Roll out the dough to about 1/5" thickness.

  5. Cut out various shapes.

  6. Place on a baking sheet on top of parchment paper.

  7. Bake for about 15 minuts.

  8. Roll cookies in cinemon/sugar mixture.
