Meringues

All Bakery Recipes

Ingredients

Switch to Steps
  • 4 Large Egg Whites (room temperature)
  • 1/2 Tsp Lemon Juice
  • Pinch of Salt`
  • 1 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla Extract

Steps

Switch to Ingredients
  1. Preheat oven to 225F.

  2. Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.

  3. Combine egg whites, lemon juics and salt in a large,
    completely clean, completely grease-free bowl.

  4. Using an electric mixer, stir on low speed until mixture becomes foamy.

  5. Increase speed to high and gradually add sugar,
    about 1 Tbsp at a time, stirring after each addition
    until sugar is dissolved (about 15-20 seconds between additions).

  6. Beat until mixture is thick, shiny, and has increased in volume.

  7. Mixture should have stiff peaks and sugar should be completely dissolved.
    and simmer until soft, about 10 minutes.

  8. Stir in vanilla extract.

  9. Place the mixture in cookie-sized portions on the baking sheet.

  10. Return the apricots to the saucepan and add the lemon rind, granulated sugar,
    corn starch, and 4 Tbsp of the soaking liquid.

  11. Bake for 1 hour.

  12. Turn off the oven once the baking time has passed,
    and do not open then oven.

  13. Leave the oven door closed and allow the cookies to cool
    completely in the oven (1-2 hours) before removing.
