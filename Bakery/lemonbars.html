Lemon Bars

All Bakery Recipes

Ingredients

Switch to Steps
  • 1/2 cup Powdered Sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup Flour
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 3/4 cup Butter
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Freshly Squeezed Lemon Juice
  • 3/4 cup Whipping Cream
  • 3 Lemons

Steps

Switch to Ingredients
  1. Preheat oven to 325F.

  2. Grease 13"x9" baking pan.

  3. Sift the powdered sugar, flour and salt into a bowl.

  4. With a pastry blender, cut in the butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  5. Press the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan.

  6. Bake until golden brown, about 20 minutes.

  7. Meanwhile prepare lemon rind.

  8. Then, for the topping, whisk the eggs and sugar together until blended.

  9. Add the lemon rind and juice and mix well.

  10. Lightly whip the cream and fold into the egg mixture.

  11. Pour over the still warm crust, return to the oven and bake until set, about 40 minutes.

  12. Cool completely before cutting into bars.

  13. Dust with powdered sugar.
