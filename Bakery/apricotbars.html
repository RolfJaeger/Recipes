Apricot Bars

All Bakery Recipes

Ingredients

Switch to Steps
  • 1/2 cup Brown Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Flour
  • 6 Tbsp Unsalted Butter
  • 1 cup Dried Apricots
  • 1 cup Water
  • 1 Lemon
  • 1/3 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 2 tsp Corn Starch
  • 1/2 cup Walnuts

Steps

Switch to Ingredients
  1. Preheat oven to 325F.

  2. In a bowl, combine the brown sugar and flour.

  3. With a pastry blender, cut in the butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  4. Press the mixture into the bottom of 8" square pan.

  5. Bake for 15 minutes.

  6. Remove from oven, but leave the oven on.

  7. Meanwhile, for the topping, combine the apricots and water in a saucepan
    and simmer until soft, about 10 minutes.

  8. Strain the liquid and reserve.

  9. Chop the apricots.

  10. Return the apricots to the saucepan and add the lemon rind, granulated sugar,
    corn starch, and 4 Tbsp of the soaking liquid.

  11. Cook for 1 minute.

  12. Cool slightly before spreading the topping over the base.
    Sprinkle the walnuts on top and continue baking for 20 minutes more.

  13. Let cool in the pan before cutting into bars
