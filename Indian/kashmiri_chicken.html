Kashmir Chicken Curry

All Curry Recipes

Ingredients

Switch to Steps
  • 4 tsp Kashmir Masala Paste
  • 4 Tbsp Tomato Ketchup
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 tsp Five-Spice Powder
  • 1 tsp Granulated Sugar
  • 8 Chicken Joints, skinned
  • 3 Tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 2 in Piece of Fresh Ginger, finely chopped
  • 4 Garlic Cloves, crushed
  • Juice of 1 Lemon
  • 1 Tbsp Fresh Cilantro Leaves, finely chopped

Steps

Switch to Ingredients

Marinae

  1. Mix the masala paste, tomato ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, five-spice powder, salt and sugar.

  2. Allow the mixture to rest in a warm place until the sugar has dissolved.

Chicken

  1. Rub the chicken pieces with the marinade and allow to rest in a cool place
    for 2 hours or in the refrigerator overnight.

  2. Bring to room temperature before cooking.

  3. Heat the oil in a wok or large pan.

  4. Fry HALF the ginger and ALL the garlic until golden.

  5. Add the chicken and fry until both sides are sealed.

  6. Cover and cook until the chicken is tender, and the oil has separated from the sauce.

  7. Sprinkle the chicken with the lemon juice, remaining ginger and chopped cilantro Leaves
    and mix well.

  8. Serve hot.
