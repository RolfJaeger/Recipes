Tandoori Chicken

All Curry Recipes

Ingredients

Switch to Steps
  • 3 lb Oven-Ready Chicken
  • 8 oz Plain Yogurt, beaten
  • 4 Tbsp Tandoori Masala Paste
  • 2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • Salt
  • Lemon Slice and Onion Rings to garnish

Steps

Switch to Ingredients
  1. Using a small, sharp knife or scissors, remove the skin from the chicken and trim off any excess fat.

  2. Using a fork prick the flesh at random.

  3. Cut the chicken in half down the center and through the breast.
    Cut each piece in half again.
    Make a few deep gashes diagonally into the flesh.

  4. Mix the yogurt with the masala paste and season with salt.

  5. Spread the chicken with the yogurt mixture, spreading some into the gashes.

  6. Leave to marinate in a cool place for at least 2 hours, or in the refrigerator overnight.

  7. Preheat the oven to 475F.

  8. Place the chicken quarters on a wire rack in a deep baking tray.

  9. Spread the chicken with any excess marinade,
    reserving a little for basting halfway through the cooking time.

  10. Melt the vegetable oil and pour over the chicken pieces to seal the surface.
    This helps keep the center moist during roasting.

  11. Roast the chicken for about 10 minutes, then remove from the oven, leaving the oven on.

  12. Baste the chicken with the remaining marinade.

  13. Return to the oven and switch off the heat.

  14. Leave the chicken in the oven for 15-20 minutes without opening the door.

  15. Serve on a bed of lettuce and garnish with the lemon and the onion rings.
