Chicken in Cashew Nut Sauce

All Curry Recipes

Ingredients

Switch to Steps
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 1/3 cup Cashew Nuts
  • 1 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Crushed Garlic
  • 1 tsp Chilli Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1/4 tsp Ground Turmeric
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 Tbsp Plain Yogurt
  • 2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Tbsp Fresh Cilantro, chopped
  • 1 Tbsp Golden Raisins (Sultanas)
  • 1 lb Chicken Breast Fillets, skinned and cubed
  • 6 oz White Mushrooms
  • 1 1/4 cup Water

Steps

Switch to Ingredients
  1. Cut the onion into quartersm then place in a food processor or blender.
    Process for 1 minute.

  2. Add the tomato paste, cashew nuts, garam masala, crushed garlic,
    chilli powder, lemon juice, turmeric, salt and yogurt to the onions
    and process for a further 1 - 1 1/2 minutes.

  3. In a wok or large pan, heat the oil, lower the heat to medium
    and pour in the onion and spice mixture.

  4. Fry gently, stirring frequently, for about 2 minutes, lowering the heat if necessary.

  5. Add the fresh cilantro, sultanas and cubed chicken to the pan
    and continue to stir-fry, for a further minute.

  6. Add the mushrooms, pour in the measured water and bring to a simmer.

  7. Cover the pan and cook over a low heat for about 10 minutes.

  8. After this time, check that the chicken is cooked all the way through and the sauce is thick.
    Continue to cook for a little longer if necessary.

  9. Transfer to a warmed serving dish and garnish with chopped fresh cilantro.
