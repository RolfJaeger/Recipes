Almond-Crusted Chicken

All Chicken Recipes

Ingredients

Switch to Steps
Chicken
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/4 cup Seasoned Breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup Almonds
  • 4 Boneless, Skinless Chicken Breasts
  • 5 oz Grated Parmesan Cheese
Sauce
  • 1 tsp Minced Garlic
  • 1/3 cup Finely Chopped Onions
  • 2 Tpsp Oil
  • 1 cup White Wine
  • 1/4 cup Teriyaki Sauce

Steps

Switch to Ingredients
  1. Preheat oven to 350F.

  2. Place egg and 1 tsp water in shallow bowl and beat.

  3. In another shallow bowl, combine breadcrumbs and almonds.


  4. Dip each chicken breast in egg, then in almond mixture and place in greased 9"x13" baking pan.

  5. Bake uncovered for 20 minutes.

  6. Remove chicken from oven and sprinkle Parmesan cheese over each breast.

  7. Return to oven and bake another 15 minutes or until almonds and cheese are golden brown.

  8. In saucepan, saute garlic and onion in oil.

  9. Add wine and teriyaki sauce and bring to boil.

  10. Reduce heat and simmer for about 10 minutes or until mixture is reduced to half.

  11. WHen serving, divide sauce among 4 plates and place chicken breasts on top.
