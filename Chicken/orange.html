Orange-Spiced Chicken

All Chicken Recipes

Ingredients

Switch to Steps
  • 2/3 cup Flour
  • 1/2 tsp Dried Basil
  • 1/4 tsp Tarragon Leaf
  • 2 Tbsp Oil
  • 6 large Chicken Breasts
  • 6 oz Orange Juice (preferably frozen)
  • 1/2 cup White Wine Vinegar
  • 2/3 cup Brown Sugar
  • 6 oz Long-Grain Rice

Steps

Switch to Ingredients
  1. Preheat oven to 350F.


  2. Mix flour, 1 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper and spices in a baggie.


  3. Pour oil into a large skillet and heat up.


  4. Coat chicken in flour mixture and brown both sides.


  5. In small bowl, mix orange juice, 1/4 cup water, vinegar and brown sugar.

  6. When chicken is brown, place it in sprayed baking dish, cover with orange juice mixture and bake uncovered for 1 hour.

  7. Serve chicken and orange sauce over rice.
