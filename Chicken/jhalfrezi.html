Chicken Jhalfrezi

All Chicken Recipes

Ingredients

Switch to Steps
  • 1 1/2 lb Chicken Breasts or Thighs
  • 2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 tsp Whole Cumin Seed
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper, chopped
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper, chopped
  • 1 Garlic Clove, crushed
  • 1 3/4" Ginger Root
  • 1 Tbsp Curry Paste
  • 1/4 tsp Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Ground Coriander Seed
  • 1 tsp Ground Cumin Seed
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 4 oz Chopped Tomatoes (from can)
  • 2 Tbsp Fresh Coriander, chopped

Steps

Switch to Ingredients
  1. Cut chicken into 1" cubes.

  2. Heat oil in a wok or large pan and fry the cumin seeds for 30-40 seconds until they begin to splutter.

  3. Add the onion, garlic and ginger and fry for 6-8 minutes.

  4. Add the curry paste and stir-fry for about 2 minutes.

  5. Stir in the chili powder, ground coriander, cumin and salt and add 1 Tbsp water. Stir-fry for a further 2 minutes.

  6. Add the chicken and stir-fry for about 5 minutes.

  7. Add the tomatoes and fresh coriander and cook covered for about 15 minutes, until the chicken is tender.

  8. Optionally: garnish with fresh coriander.
