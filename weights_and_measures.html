Weight and Measures

1 tsp = 8 drops
1 Tbsp = 3 tsp
4 Tbsp = 1/4 cup
5 1/3 Tbsp = 1/3 cup
8 Tbsp = 1/2 cup
16 Tbsp = 1 cup (dry)
1 fl. oz = 2 Tbsp
16 oz = 1 pound
1 cup (liquid) = 1/2 pint
2 cups (16 oz) = 1 pint
2 pints (4 cups) = 1 quart
4 quarts = 1 gallon


U.S. Europe
1 tsp = 1 coffee spoon
1 Tbsp = 1 soup spoon
2 Tbsp = 1 English Tbsp
2 tsp = 1 English tsp
20 fl oz = 1 English pint
10 oz = 1 English cup
