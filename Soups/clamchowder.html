Clam Chowder

All Soup Recipes


Ingredients

Switch to Steps
  • 3 Strips of Smoked Bacon
  • 1 Onion
  • 6 1/2 oz Minced Clams
  • 1 can Clam Juice
  • 2 Red Potatoes
  • 3/4 cup Corn
  • 1 1/2 cup Milk
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 3/4 tsp Dried Savory Leaves

Steps

Switch to Ingredients
  1. Cook the bacon in a 3-qt saucepan until browned, about 6 minutes.

  2. Add the onion to the pan, and saute until translucent, about 3 minutes.

  3. Drain the clams, reserving the juice.

  4. Add the canned clam juice, the potatoes, and the reserved juice to the pan.

  5. Cook the mixture until the potatoes are tender, about 15 to 20 minutes.

  6. Using a slotted spoon, transfer half the mixture to a bowl, cover with foil to keep warm.

  7. Using a handheld immersion blender, puree the onion mixture in the pan.

  8. Return the reserved vegetabled to the pan.

  9. Stir in the corn, milk, Worcestershire sauce, savory leaves and clams.

  10. Heat the chowder on low heat (do not boil!),
    stirring occasionally, until it is hot, 5-10 minutes.

  11. Crumble the bacon and top each serving with it.
